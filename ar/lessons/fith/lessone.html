Typing Lesson 5: Punctuation

If you have completed the first four lessons, pat yourself on the back for coming this far! You are now well on your way to becoming an excellent typist.In this lesson we will learn how to type commonly used punctuation marks. You have already learned the period, comma and semicolon, and we will review those keys as well. In the previous lesson, we learned to type capital letters. The capital letters are the secondary characters for the lower case keys and holding the shift allows us to type them. Likewise, the symbol keys also have other symbols as secondary characters, and you will encounter several of these keys in this lesson.
